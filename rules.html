PIONEER ELECTRONICS (USA) INC.
APPRADIO HACKATHON CONTEST
Official Rules

• CONTEST: The Pioneer Electronics (USA) Inc. AppRadio Hackathon Contest (“Contest”) will take place on October 30, 2012, beginning at 10:00 am (Pacific Time) during the SEMA Show 2012 at the Las Vegas Convention Center, 3150 Paradise Road, Las Vegas, NV 89109.  The administrator of this contest and the sponsor of this contest is Pioneer Electronics (USA) Inc. (“Sponsor”) located at 1925 E. Dominguez St., Long Beach, CA 90810.

• HOW TO ENTER: Prior to the start time indicated above, go www.pioneerhackathon.com and follow the Register Now link to the http://pioneerhackathonvegas.eventbrite.com.  Once you are registered, go to the Pioneer AppRadio Hackathon event located in the Las Vegas Convention Center, South Hall, Upper Lobby, booth #33000, October 30, 2012 at 10:00 a.m. (Pacific Time) to compete.  You can recruit up to nine people to join your team or teams may be assigned by Sponsor at the event.  All team members must meet eligibility requirements for the team to win a prize.  Between 12:00 p.m. (Pacific Time) on Tuesday, October 30, 2012 and 8:59 a.m. (Pacific Time) on Friday, November 2, 2012, your team will be asked to create a mobile application software proposal and business plan (“Submission”). Submissions must be suitable for in-vehicle use and formatted to be compatible with the Pioneer App Radio Platform. There will be three categories of competition tracks that your team can select from, described below. Teams may participate in multiple categories, but each Submission must be unique. Beginning at 9:00 am Pacific Time on Friday, November 2, 2012, each submission will be reviewed by the Sponsor. Your team may be asked to demonstrate your Submission and provide an oral presentation to the Sponsor.  The number of contestants may be limited due to time constraints.  Any additional requirements concerning Submissions will be provided to you at the time of the Contest.

 

Category 1: Voice Interface SNS application.

• Develop a mobile application software proposal and business plan for safe in-vehicle use of Social Networking Services (SNS) through a voice recognition and text-to-speech interface.

Category 2: Location Based Services application.

• Develop a mobile application software proposal and business plan for geo-fenced or geo-tagged automotive related location based services (LBS).

Category 3: SEMA Member automotive accessory application.

• Develop a mobile application software proposal and business plan that incorporates one or more SEMA Show 2012 exhibitor’s technology, products or services.

• ELIGIBILITY:  The Contest is open to legal residents of the 50 United States and the District of Columbia, ages 18 or older.  Employees, officers and directors of Sponsor, Administrator, any company providing technical support at the Contest for the Submission and any prize supplier, and their respective parent companies, subsidiaries and affiliates and their advertising, promotion, and fulfillment agencies (the “Contest Parties”) are not eligible to enter or win.  Immediate family members and household members of such individuals are also not eligible to enter or win.  “Immediate family members” means parents, step-parents, legal guardians, children, step-children, siblings, step-siblings, or spouses.  “Household members” means those people who share the same residence at least three months a year.  Subject to all applicable laws.  Void outside the U.S. and where prohibited.

• JUDGING/WINNER SELECTION:  Submissions and oral presentation of Submission will be judged by a panel of judges, chosen by the Sponsor. The Submission will be judged upon the judge’s criteria, which includes, but is not limited to:  (1) Adherence to category; (2) Originality of the idea; (3) Ability to articulately present the application; (4) Technology used in the application and (5) User Interface design and adherence to provided guidelines for screen resolution and orientation.  In the event of a tie, the winning team will be determined by the highest score for originality of idea.  A team can win only one prize per category.
